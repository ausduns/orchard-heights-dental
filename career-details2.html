Dental Hygienist

At Dental, we’re more than just a dental practice — we’re a community dedicated to transforming smiles and improving lives.

Job description


At Dental, we are passionate about creating healthy, confident smiles in a welcoming and supportive environment. Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional care with a patient-first approach. We’re looking for a skilled and compassionate Dental Hygienist to join our growing team and help us deliver top-notch oral care.


Key responsibilities

  • Perform dental cleanings and preventive care procedures with precision and care.


  • Educate patients on oral hygiene practices and post-treatment care.


  • Conduct periodontal assessments and provide non-surgical periodontal therapy as needed.


  • Collaborate with dentists and other team members to ensure optimal patient care.


  • Maintain accurate patient records and follow infection control protocols.


Qualifications

  • Licensed Dental Hygienist.


  • Strong knowledge of dental hygiene procedures and equipment.


  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.


  • Passion for patient care and education.


  • Detail-oriented and organized with a commitment to maintaining a safe and sterile environment.


What we offer

  • Competitive salary and benefits package.


  • Opportunities for professional development and continuing education.


  • A positive, team-oriented work environment.


  • State-of-the-art facilities and technology.


  • Flexible scheduling options.
