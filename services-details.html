What is Preventative Care?

Preventative dental care is the modern way of helping you keep a healthy mouth. It helps you keep your teeth, and means you need to have less dental treatment. The two main causes of tooth loss are decay and gum disease. The better you prevent or deal with these two problems, the more chance you will have of keeping your teeth for life.
